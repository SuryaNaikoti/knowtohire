# Sprint 9 Kickoff Plan: AI & Automation

Sprint 9 moves KnowToHire into the AI and Automation phase. We will leverage deep language processing and semantic models to match candidate talents with recruiter job needs.

## Objectives & Work Phases

- [ ] **AI Resume Analyzer:** Automated extraction of candidate experiences, matching profiles with key credentials.
- [ ] **AI Job Matching Engine:** Rank applicants based on skills overlap and historical candidate data.
- [ ] **Interview Simulator & Prep:** Dynamic AI question bank generator tailored per role.
- [ ] **Semantic Job Search:** Embed semantic vector indices in Postgres using pgvector.
- [ ] **AI Career Assistant:** Interactive chatbot assisting candidate applications.
